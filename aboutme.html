Why do I do this?

Hi Folks, my name is Josh and I am a music enthusiast. I am also a husband, father, and at times a philosipher.

The reason I started this page is because I have taken up playing guitar and realize there can be a steep learning curve.

For those with the same struggles, I would like to take you along on my journey in hopes we both come out enriched, more personally developed and most of all

a better guitar player. Learning something new rarely comes easy but I think with the right tools and resources we can achieve our guitar playing goals together.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website and hope that my journey can help, encourage, and teach you to be a better guitar player.

Also feel free to reach out to me via my contact page or you can also subscribe to my newsletter for music trends, tips, and information about my upcoming book.

Sincerely,

-Josh
